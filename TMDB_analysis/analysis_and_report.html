Introduction¶

The Movie Database provides data about 10000 movies along with their genres, popularity, budgets, revenues and more.
I plan to explore the

  • Average duration of movies by year
  • Popular genres from year to year
  • Popular production companies over the years
  • Factors that possibly influence movies with high revenues

Conclusions¶

Findings¶

  • The average movie runtime has decreased over the years
  • Only 2 genres have been the most popular for the 55 years span - Comedy and Drama. Laughter is afterall the best medicine.
  • 11 companies have produced the most films from 1960 to 2015. They are Allied Artists Pictures, Alta Vista Productions, Columbia Pictures Corporation, Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer (MGM), Miramax Films, Paramount Pictures, Relativity Media, TriStar Pictures, Twentieth Century Fox Film Corporation, United Artists, Universal Pictures, Walt Disney Pictures and Warner Bros.
  • All the numerical variables are positively correlated with the revenue generated. However, most of the relationships are weak and do not necessarily cause revenue outcome.

Limitations¶

  • I assumed the maximum number of genres and production companies for each movie to be 5.
